Are you looking for an Architect or Designer? BR Architecture is based in North Wales and Chester and will guide you through the entire design process for new build, conversions, or extensions projects.

BR Architecture is a family run Business which has been running for 16 years. Barry Richards has over 20 years of experience in Architecture and has worked with a number of local and national architects practices, on both small and large scale projects throughout North Wales, Cheshire and the North West.
